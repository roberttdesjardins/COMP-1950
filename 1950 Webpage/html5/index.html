City Travel Guide

Berlin

Berlin is a wonderful travel desination with something to see and do for every type of traveler. Make sure to explore the variety of beers which Germany is famous for and vist historical sites such as the Brandenburg Gate, Checkpoint Charlie, and the Berlin Wall. Outside the city explore the castles and smaller villages for a unique and unforgettable experience.

  • Brandenburg Gate
  • German Beer
  • German Castles

Rating: 9/10

London

London is an interesting city with lots of culture and sites. That said, my experience was less than spectacular. London is a very expensive city so be prepared to spend a lot for food, shopping, and accommodations. Additionally, the city is very dirty for a world class city and constantly smells of urine. In the summertime pollen is abundant. That said, if you choose to visit, make sure to attend some of the theatre plays such as Lion King and Wicked.

  • London Sites
  • London Plays

Rating: 3/10

Barcelona

Barcelona is a great city to visit for the local cuisine, sites, and to experience Spanish culture. Make sure to visit Gaudí's works including the famous Sagrada Familia. For football fans, catch a game at Camp Nou. The city is relatively inexpensive.

  • Gaudí's Works
  • Spanish Cuisine

Rating: 6/10

Athens

Athens is a wonderful city to visit for those who appreciate history. Athens has some of the most historically significant sites on earth, amazing food, and unique culture. Be sure to check out the Parthenon, Acropolis, and local museums. Don't forget to eat the local food and wine. Personal recommendations include Moussaka and lamb chops.

  • Athens Must See Sites
  • Greek Cuisine

Rating: 8/10
