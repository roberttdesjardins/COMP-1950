EM and REM

EM and REM are relative measurements that can be especially useful in responsive designs. Note that each of them will inherit their sizing differently. In general, EM proves most useful, since if a parent changes in size, you usually desire its children to adjust their size proportionally. The 'R' in REM stands for 'Root', describing how it will use the <html> root tag for relative sizing.

EM values inherit from their nearest parent or ancestor.
